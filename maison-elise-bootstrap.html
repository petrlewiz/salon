About

Crafted by Ines Maria

With over a decade of experience in editorial and salon work, Ines brings a thoughtful eye and quiet precision to every appointment. Trained in Paris and Milan, she specialises in lived-in colour, effortless cuts, and restorative treatments.

The studio is a calm, intentional space — no rushed appointments, no distractions. Just skilled hands and a genuine conversation about what you want.
